about
Ashen Spire is a fast-paced dungeon-crawler action game where you battle through frozen ruins using one of three unique classes: the Swordsman's melee slash, the Mage's arcane beams, or the Archer's precise volleys. Defeat increasingly challenging enemies, epic bosses like the Golem and Yeti, and crush destructible environments to collect loot and upgrades. Master your stamina resources, discover hidden treasures, and progress through multiple challenging maps in solo adventure or cooperative multiplayer. Will you escape the Spire?
